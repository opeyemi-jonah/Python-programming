Open a file: 
    open(file_name, mode, buffering)
mode & buffering are optional.

Mode values: 
'r' = read mode, 
'w' = write mode, 
'a' = append mode, 
'b' = binary mode (added to other mode), 
'+' = read/write mode (added to other mode).
